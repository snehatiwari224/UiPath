ABOUT US

The UiPath Student Community at AITR was established in the year 2022 with the aim of fostering knowledge and skills in the field of Robotic Process Automation (RPA). Led by dedicated individuals, this community has grown steadily and has become a hub for students interested in UiPath and RPA technologies.

EVENTS

RPA INTRODUCTION

The "RPA Introduction" event served as a stepping stone for students who were new to Robotic Process Automation. Practical demonstrations and hands-on sessions allowed participants to get a firsthand experience of creating simple automation workflows using UiPath Studio.

Hack A Bot

"Hack A Bot" was an exciting and competitive event that brought out the creative and problem-solving skills of the participants. Teams were challenged to design and implement innovative bots to solve specific business scenarios. The event not only tested technicalproficiency but also encouraged collaboration and teamwork among the participants.The judging criteria included functionality, efficiency, and the overall impact of the develop bots on process automation.

CONCLUSION

In conclusion, the UiPath Student Community at AITR stands as a testament to the collaborative spirit and passion for technology among its members. The diverse range of events, including the enlightening RPA Introduction and the stimulating Hack A Bot, has not only enriched the skill sets of its members but has also fostered a culture of creativity and problem-solving.
